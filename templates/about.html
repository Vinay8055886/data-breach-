Our Mission

BreachGuard was built with one goal: to give everyday users instant visibility into whether their personal information — emails, phone numbers, and more — has been exposed in any known data breach. We believe everyone deserves to know if their data is at risk.

⚙️

How It Works

  • 1️⃣
    Enter Your Data

    Type in your email address or phone number in the search box.

  • 2️⃣
    We Scan Our Database

    Our engine cross-references your input against thousands of known breach records.

  • 3️⃣
    Instant Results

    Within seconds, you get a clear report showing if your data was found in any breach.
